# Panther Creek State Park

## Why Panther Creek?

**Cherokee Lake** in the Morristown area — 1,435 acres on a forested ridge overlooking the reservoir. Point Lookout provides sweeping lake views for raptor and waterfowl scanning. Winter Bald Eagles roost along the lake.

## Birding

- **Bald Eagle** — winter residents on Cherokee Lake
- **Osprey** — spring/summer fishing
- **Northern Parula** — ridge forest canopy
- **Winter waterfowl** in lake coves
- **Great Blue Heron** — shoreline year-round

## Logistics

- **Entry**: Free
- **Camping**: 50 sites
- **Trails**: 15 miles
- **eBird hotspot**: Panther Creek SP

**Best strategy**: Winter morning — Point Lookout for eagles + lake scan. 2 hours.
