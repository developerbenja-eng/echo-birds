# Cove Lake State Park

## Why Cove Lake?

A small park at the **foot of the Cumberland Mountains** with a scenic lake and mountain-edge forest. The lake attracts waterfowl in winter and the forested ridges support breeding warblers.

## Birding

- **Belted Kingfisher** — lake resident
- **Northern Parula** — lakeside forest canopy
- **Winter waterfowl** on Cove Lake
- **Pileated Woodpecker** — forest ridges
- **Eastern Phoebe** — lake structures

## Logistics

- **Entry**: Free
- **Camping**: 106 sites
- **Trails**: 5 miles
- **Restaurant**: Park restaurant overlooking lake
- **eBird hotspot**: Cove Lake SP

**Best strategy**: Lake walk + forest trail. 1-2 hours. Stop en route to other parks.

## Pairing

- **Norris Dam** (30 min south) — larger TVA park
- **Indian Mountain** (20 min north) — KY border park
