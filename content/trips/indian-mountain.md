# Indian Mountain State Park

## Why Indian Mountain?

A **small park at the Kentucky border** in Jellico — 213 acres with a lake and mountain-edge forest. Built on reclaimed strip mine land. Not a birding destination, but a pleasant stop.

## Birding

- **Northern Parula** — lakeside forest
- **Eastern Towhee** — brushy edges
- **Belted Kingfisher** — lake
- **Eastern Phoebe** — structures near lake

## Logistics

- **Entry**: Free
- **Camping**: 49 sites
- **Lake**: Small, fishing-focused
- **eBird hotspot**: Indian Mountain SP

**Best strategy**: Quick stop. 30-60 minutes. Near I-75 at the KY border.
