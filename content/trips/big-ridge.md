# Big Ridge State Park

## Why Big Ridge?

A **CCC-era park** on Norris Lake with 3,687 acres of ridge-and-valley forest. Quieter and less developed than Norris Dam — good for unhurried forest birding. The CCC-era structures (dam, stone lodge) are historically significant.

## Birding

- **Northern Parula** — canopy above lake trail
- **Hooded Warbler** — dense understory in ravines
- **Great Crested Flycatcher** — WHEEP from canopy
- **Pileated Woodpecker** — mature forest
- **Winter waterfowl** on lake cove

## Logistics

- **Entry**: Free
- **Camping**: 50 sites + rustic cabins
- **Trails**: 10+ miles
- **eBird hotspot**: Big Ridge SP

**Best strategy**: Morning forest trail + lake walk. 2-3 hours. Pair with Norris Dam.
