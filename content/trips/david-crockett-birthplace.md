# David Crockett Birthplace State Park

## Why David Crockett Birthplace?

Where **Davy Crockett was born** in 1786 — on the banks of the **Nolichucky River** in far-east Tennessee. A small park (105 acres) with a replica cabin, museum, and river access.

The Nolichucky is a **beautiful mountain river** flowing out of the Blue Ridge — the riparian habitat supports river-corridor species.

## Birding

- **Belted Kingfisher** — Nolichucky River
- **Warbling Vireo** — sycamores along the river
- **Great Blue Heron** — river shallows
- **Louisiana Waterthrush** — rocky stream edges
- **Eastern Phoebe** — bridge/cabin areas
