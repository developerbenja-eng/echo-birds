# South Cumberland State Park

## Why South Cumberland?

**30,000 acres** — Tennessee's largest state park by total acreage, spread across **multiple non-contiguous areas** along the southern Cumberland Plateau escarpment. Includes Grundy Forest, Fiery Gizzard, Stone Door, and connects to Savage Gulf. The deep gorges host **Swainson's Warbler** — one of the rarest and most sought-after breeding warblers in Tennessee.

## The landscape

- **Plateau escarpment** — dramatic cliff edges with 1,000-ft drops
- **Deep gorges** with hemlock, rhododendron, and rich cove forest
- **Fiery Gizzard Creek** — rugged gorge with waterfalls
- **Grundy Forest** — old-growth hemlock + hardwood

## Birding highlights

### Grundy Forest Day Loop (2 mi — moderate)
Through old-growth forest with waterfalls.

- **Swainson's Warbler** — dense rhododendron thickets in gorge. Listen for the loud, ringing song
- **Louisiana Waterthrush** — gorge streams
- **Worm-eating Warbler** — steep slopes
- **Hooded Warbler** — dense understory

### Fiery Gizzard Trail (12.5 mi point-to-point — strenuous)
Epic gorge trail. Bird the first few miles.

- **Cerulean Warbler** — gorge-rim canopy
- **Black-throated Green Warbler** — hemlock zones
- **Wood Thrush** — cove forest

### Stone Door
Dramatic cliff overlook.

- **Cerulean Warbler** — rim-top canopy
- **Raven** — nesting on cliffs
- **Broad-winged Hawk** — soaring above plateau

## Seasonal strategy

**May–June** (★★★★★): Peak breeding. Swainson's Warbler singing. All species active.

**April** (★★★★): Early breeders. Migration adds diversity.

**October** (★★★): Fall foliage from cliff overlooks.

## Logistics

- **Hours**: Dawn to dusk
- **Entry**: Free
- **Camping**: Backcountry camping on Fiery Gizzard Trail
- **Trails**: 90+ miles across all units
- **eBird hotspot**: South Cumberland SP — Grundy Forest

**Best strategy**: Grundy Forest Day Loop at dawn for Swainson's Warbler. 3 hours.

## Pairing

- **Savage Gulf** (adjacent) — deepest gorges
- **Fall Creek Falls** (40 min north) — waterfalls + Cerulean
